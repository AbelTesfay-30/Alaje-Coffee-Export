Processed & Packed Coffee

Premium Ethiopian coffee, expertly processed and professionally packaged for export. Our products meet international quality standards and are ready for distribution to global markets.

Vacuum Sealed Bags

Premium Quality

Package Sizes: 250g, 500g, 1kg
Material: Multi-layer aluminum foil
Features: One-way valve, resealable zipper
Shelf Life: 12-18 months

Our vacuum-sealed bags preserve freshness and aroma by preventing oxygen exposure. The one-way valve allows CO2 to escape while keeping air out, ensuring your coffee stays fresh from roasting to brewing.

Bulk Export Bags

Wholesale

Package Sizes: 5kg, 10kg, 25kg, 60kg
Material: Jute or GrainPro bags
Features: Moisture barrier, breathable
Ideal For: Importers, roasters, distributors

Perfect for bulk orders, our export bags are designed for international shipping. GrainPro liners provide excellent moisture protection, while traditional jute bags offer breathability and sustainability.

Retail Ready Packaging

Custom Branding

Material: Kraft paper with window
Features: Degassing valve, custom labels
Customization: Your brand, our coffee

We offer white-label and custom branding services. Our retail-ready packaging is designed to stand out on shelves while maintaining the highest quality standards for coffee preservation.

Ground Coffee Packaging

Ready to Brew

Grind Options: Fine, medium, coarse

Pre-ground coffee packaged immediately after grinding and nitrogen-flushed to preserve freshness. Available in various grind sizes to suit different brewing methods, from espresso to French press.
