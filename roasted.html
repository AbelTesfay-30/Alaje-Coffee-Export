Roasted Coffee Samples

Experience the art of coffee roasting with our expertly crafted roast profiles. Each roast level brings out unique characteristics of Ethiopian coffee, from bright and fruity light roasts to rich and bold dark roasts.

Light Roast

Bright & Fruity

Roast Temperature: 356°F - 401°F (180°C - 205°C)
Color: Light brown, no oil on surface
Characteristics: Bright acidity, complex flavors
Best For: Pour over, drip coffee

Light roasts preserve the original characteristics of the coffee bean, showcasing the unique terroir and processing methods. Expect vibrant floral and fruity notes with a tea-like body. Perfect for those who appreciate the nuanced flavors of specialty Ethiopian coffee.

Medium Roast

Balanced & Sweet

Roast Temperature: 410°F - 428°F (210°C - 220°C)
Color: Medium brown, dry surface
Characteristics: Balanced flavor, medium body
Best For: All brewing methods

Medium roasts offer the perfect balance between origin characteristics and roast development. You'll experience a harmonious blend of fruity notes, chocolate sweetness, and caramel undertones. The most versatile roast level, suitable for any brewing method.

Medium-Dark Roast

Rich & Bold

Roast Temperature: 437°F - 446°F (225°C - 230°C)
Color: Dark brown, slight oil sheen
Characteristics: Full body, bittersweet flavor
Best For: Espresso, French press

Medium-dark roasts develop deeper caramelization, creating rich chocolate and toasted nut flavors while maintaining some origin character. The slight oil on the surface indicates fuller body and lower acidity, perfect for espresso-based drinks.

Dark Roast

Bold & Intense

Roast Temperature: 464°F+ (240°C+)
Color: Very dark brown to black, oily surface
Characteristics: Bold, smoky, low acidity
Best For: Espresso, cold brew

Dark roasts emphasize roast character over origin, delivering bold, smoky flavors with notes of dark chocolate and caramelized sugar. The oils on the surface create a full, heavy body with minimal acidity. Ideal for those who prefer strong, intense coffee.
