Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum order quantity?

Our minimum order quantity varies depending on the product and destination. For green beans, we typically require a minimum of 100kg. Contact us to discuss your specific needs.

Do you provide samples?

Yes! We offer sample packs for potential buyers to evaluate our coffee quality before placing larger orders. Sample costs and shipping fees apply.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept international wire transfers, letters of credit (L/C), and other secure payment methods. Payment terms can be discussed based on order size and relationship.

How long does shipping take?

Shipping times vary by destination. Typically, sea freight takes 4-8 weeks, while air freight takes 5-10 days. We provide tracking information for all shipments.

Can you provide custom roasting?

Absolutely! We offer custom roasting services tailored to your specifications. Share your desired roast profile, and we'll work to match it perfectly.

Do you offer private labeling?

Yes, we provide white-label and private labeling services. You can have our premium Ethiopian coffee packaged under your own brand with custom designs.
