Natural Ethiopian Coffee Beans

Ethiopia is the birthplace of coffee, and our natural beans showcase the unique terroir of each region. From the floral notes of Yirgacheffe to the bold flavors of Harar, experience the authentic taste of Ethiopian coffee heritage.

Yirgacheffe

Natural Process

Origin: Yirgacheffe, Southern Ethiopia
Flavor Notes: Floral, citrus, bergamot, jasmine
Aroma: Sweet, fruity, with hints of blueberry
Body: Light to medium, tea-like
Acidity: Bright and vibrant

Known worldwide for its distinctive floral and citrus characteristics, Yirgacheffe coffee is grown at high altitudes (1,700-2,200m) and is considered one of the finest coffees in the world.

Sidamo

Washed Process

Origin: Sidamo Region, Southern Ethiopia
Flavor Notes: Berry, chocolate, caramel, wine-like
Aroma: Complex, fruity, with floral undertones
Body: Medium to full
Acidity: Balanced and smooth

Sidamo coffee offers a perfect balance of sweetness and complexity. Grown in the fertile highlands, these beans deliver a rich, full-bodied cup with remarkable depth.

Harar

Natural Process

Origin: Eastern Ethiopia, Harar Region
Flavor Notes: Blueberry, dark chocolate, spice, wine
Aroma: Bold, fruity, with earthy undertones
Body: Full and heavy
Acidity: Medium to low

One of the oldest coffee-growing regions in the world, Harar produces beans with a distinctive wild, fruity character. Often described as "mocha-like" with intense blueberry notes.

Guji

Natural Process

Origin: Guji Zone, Oromia Region
Flavor Notes: Strawberry, peach, tropical fruit, honey
Aroma: Intensely fruity and sweet
Body: Medium, silky smooth
Acidity: Bright and juicy

A relatively new specialty coffee region, Guji has quickly gained recognition for producing exceptional natural coffees with explosive fruit flavors and remarkable sweetness.

Limu

Washed Process

Origin: Limu Region, Western Ethiopia
Flavor Notes: Lemon, spice, floral, wine-like
Aroma: Delicate, floral, with citrus hints
Body: Medium, balanced
Acidity: Crisp and clean

Limu coffee is known for its balanced profile and clean cup. Grown in the lush forests of western Ethiopia, these beans offer a refined and elegant coffee experience.

Jimma

Natural Process

Origin: Jimma Zone, Southwestern Ethiopia
Flavor Notes: Earthy, chocolate, nutty, fruity
Aroma: Rich, complex, with herbal notes
Body: Full and robust
Acidity: Low to medium

Jimma produces coffee with a distinctive earthy character and full body. Perfect for those who prefer a bold, traditional Ethiopian coffee with less acidity and more depth.
